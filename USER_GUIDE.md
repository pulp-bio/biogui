## The User Interface

The main window has three main areas:

**Left Side: Control Panel**

- START button: Begin recording
- STOP button: End recording
- Add Data Source button: Connect a new device
- Edit button: Modify settings
- Delete button: Remove a data source
- Data Source Tree: Shows all connected devices and their signals
- Render Length dropdown: Choose how much time to display on the graph (100ms to 10s)

**Right Side: Live Graphs**

- Shows signals in real-time as colored lines
- Y-axis: Signal strength (amplitude)
- X-axis: Time
- Each color represents a different signal or channel

## Recording Your Signals

### Before You Start

1. Check that your device is connected and powered on
2. Look at the Data Source Tree on the left – you should see your device listed
3. Make sure the signals you want are checked (✅) in the tree
4. Check the live graphs – they should show activity

## Tips for Better Results

**What works well:**

- Make sure sensors are positioned correctly on the body
- Do test recordings before capturing important data
- Check that signal quality looks good on the graphs
- Use clear, descriptive filenames

**What to avoid:**

- Don't disconnect the device during recording
- Don't use very low sampling rates unless necessary
- Don't ignore warning messages
